Bank account

You might wish to open a Dutch bank account during your stay. The major Dutch banks are ing, abn amro and Rabobank. Sustainable banks are asn bank and Triodos bank. Once you have opened an account, the bank will provide you with a debit card. Although most shops and restaurants accept cash, there is an increasing number of places that only accept debit cards.

Keep in mind that although most of the bigger stores accept credit cards, many shops do not. Be sure to always have enough cash on you – or get yourself a Dutch debit card. If all else fails, ask the shopkeeper to hold your items and run to the nearest atm to withdraw money.
